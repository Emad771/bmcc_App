Borough of Manhattan Community College (BMCC) is a public community college located in the heart of New York City. Established in 1963, BMCC is part of the City University of New York (CUNY) system and offers associate's degrees and certificate programs in a wide range of subjects.

With more than 23,000 students, BMCC is the largest community college in CUNY and one of the largest in the country. The college's diverse student body reflects the vibrant and multicultural nature of New York City, with students coming from all over the world to take advantage of BMCC's high-quality education and affordable tuition.

BMCC's main campus is located in the Tribeca neighborhood of Manhattan, and the college also has a satellite campus in downtown Brooklyn. The college's state-of-the-art facilities include modern classrooms and labs, a large library, a performing arts center, and an athletic center.

BMCC offers a wide range of academic programs, including liberal arts and sciences, business, health sciences, and technology. The college also has a strong focus on career and technical education, with programs in fields such as nursing, computer science, and criminal justice.

One of the unique aspects of BMCC is its commitment to providing students with real-world experience and hands-on learning opportunities. The college has partnerships with numerous businesses and organizations in the city, providing students with access to internships, job shadowing, and other experiential learning opportunities.

In addition to its academic programs, BMCC offers a variety of student support services, including tutoring and academic advising, career counseling, and health and wellness services. The college also has a vibrant student life, with over 50 clubs and organizations, as well as events and activities throughout the year.

Graduates of BMCC have a strong track record of success, with many going on to transfer to four-year colleges and universities or entering the workforce with the skills and knowledge they gained at BMCC. The college has a strong transfer program, with partnerships with over 50 colleges and universities in the region, making it easy for students to continue their education after earning their associate's degree at BMCC.

Overall, BMCC is a vibrant and dynamic institution that provides students with a high-quality education at an affordable price. Whether you're looking to transfer to a four-year college, enter the workforce, or simply explore your interests, BMCC has something for everyone.
